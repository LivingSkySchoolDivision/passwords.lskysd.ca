How to change your Living Sky password


  • Change your password on a School Division computer
  • Change your password via a web browser
  • If you think your account has been hacked or compromised


If you are unable to access your account

Students

  1. Any teacher, librarian, or secretary can reset student passwords
  2. If your teacher is not able to help you, have them contact the IT Help Desk for assistance.

Staff

  1. Contact the IT Help Desk by dialing 7962 from a Cisco phone in one of our schools
  2. Alternatively, ask your school's secretary or principal to submit a help desk ticket to reset your password.
    • For security reasons, ticket requests must come from a school secretary or school administration.


Change your password on a School Division computer

  1. Sign into any school division Microsoft Windows based computer.
    • This could be a teacher laptop, a student workstation, a loaner device, etc.
    • This will not work on a Chromebook.
  2. Press CTRL + ALT + DEL
  3. Choose Change a password... from the list
  4. Enter your current password in the Old Password field
  5. Enter a new password in the next two fields
  6. Click the arrow on the right side of the final password field
  7. Your password is now updated


Change your password via a web browser

  1. Go to https://portal.office.com/account#security
  2. Log into your account, if you are not automatically logged in
  3. Click Password
  4. Enter your current password in the Old Password field
  5. Enter a new password in both the Create New Password and Confirm New Password fields
  6. Click the Submit button
  7. Your password is now updated. It may take 10-15 minutes for the computers at the school to register the change.


If you think your account has been hacked or compromised

Students

If you suspect that your account has been hacked, please notify a teacher immediately, and have them change your password. Changing your password to something that it's never been set to before is the best way to keep hackers out of your account.

Staff

If you suspect that your account has been hacked, please contact the IT Help Desk by ticket, email, or dialing 7962 from any Cisco phone at any of our schools.
